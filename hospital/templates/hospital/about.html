Our Story

Building Trust Through Excellence Since 1995

MedVita Hospital was founded with a singular vision: to deliver world-class medical care with genuine human compassion. What started as a small 50-bed facility has grown into a 500-bed, multi-specialty tertiary care center.

Over 28 years, we have served more than 50,000 patients and performed over 20,000 successful surgeries. Our team of 200+ specialist doctors work together around the clock.

We are internationally accredited by JCI and recognized for excellence in patient safety and quality care.
